Step 1: Visit the IRCTC Website or Open the App

  • Website: GO TO IRCTC
  • App: Download the official IRCTC Rail Connect app from Google Play Store. Click here to get the app

Step 2: Log In to Your IRCTC Account

  • Enter your username and password.
  • If you don’t have an account, click on "Sign Up" to register.

Step 3: Plan Your Journey

  • Enter Travel Details: Select "From Station" and "To Station".
  • Choose the date of journey.
  • Select the travel class (Sleeper, AC, etc.) and quota (General, Tatkal, etc.).
  • Click "Find Trains" to see available options.

Step 4: Choose a Train

  • Browse the list of trains and check the availability status (e.g., Available, RAC, WL).
  • Click on "Book Now" next to your preferred train and class.

Step 5: Fill Passenger Details

  • Enter the following information for each passenger:
    1. Name
    2. Age
    3. Gender
    4. Berth preference (optional)
    5. Add details for a child passenger if applicable
    6. Provide a mobile number to receive ticket updates

Step 6: Select Additional Options

  • Choose if you want to book travel insurance (optional).
  • Add a meal preference if available.

Step 7: Make Payment

  • Select a payment option:
    1. Net Banking
    2. Credit/Debit Card
    3. UPI
    4. Wallets (like Paytm, PhonePe, etc.)
  • Complete the payment process.

Step 8: Confirm Booking

  • Once payment is successful, you’ll receive a Booking Confirmation on the screen. Your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email and mobile number.

Step 9: Download and Save Your Ticket

  • Save a digital copy of the ticket or print it if needed. You can access it anytime under the "Booked Tickets" section.
