FAQ's

Can I buy a ticket after boarding the train? Yes, you can approach the ticket examiner (TTE) to buy a ticket, but additional fines may apply. It's better to purchase a ticket before boarding.
What should I do if I missed buying my ticket at the station? Visit the ticket counter immediately if you haven’t boarded yet. If already on the train, inform the TTE as soon as possible.
Is there an app or online service to purchase a ticket after the train has started? Some train services allow real-time booking through official apps or websites like IRCTC (India) or similar services in other countries.
What is the penalty for traveling without a ticket? Penalties vary by country and rail authority. Generally, it includes the ticket price plus a fine.
Can I reserve a seat if I don't have a ticket? Onboard reservation might be possible for unreserved coaches if the TTE approves, but it is subject to seat availability.
Do I need identification to buy a ticket onboard? Yes, you may be required to provide valid identification, especially for reserved tickets.
Can I pay with a card or mobile payment onboard? Many railways now accept digital payments, but it's best to confirm with the TTE or railway's guidelines.
